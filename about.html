I’m Tatjana, a fashion stylist with a passion for style and design that started when I was 10 years old, flipping through InStyle magazine and dreaming of the haute couture world. I was particularly mesmerized by John Galliano’s designs at Dior.

At 20, I made the bold decision to leave the corporate world behind and follow my true calling in fashion. I gained valuable experience working with major luxury retailers, honing my skills along the way.

After becoming a wife and mother, I took my passion to the next level by launching Styled, offering personalized services to clients who share my love for fashion. Whether it’s curating wardrobes, styling for special occasions, or offering everyday chic advice, I bring a keen eye for detail and a genuine love for helping others feel their best.
